About us You’re in good company We enable companies to pay and get paid cashlessly, online and offline, adding payment, remittance and payout features to their online and offline services, with our set of simple fintech APIs
Our Goal Give our customers the peace of mind of ensured financial compliance and data security
iPSP.de GmbH is a financial technology company founded in 2011, based in Frankfurt-am-Main (Germany). We define ourselves as a payment infrastructure-on-demand company Our clients are online merchants, mobile operators, brick-and-mortar retailers, and software companies who develop application software that requires payment functionality.
Making a difference Our goal is to give our customers the peace of mind of ensured financial compliance and data security, and convenience of a one-stop-shop partnership for anything payment-related anywhere the world.
Experienced & professional Since 2019, iPSP.de GmbH is a subsidiary of CreditPilot PLC, a global financial technology company specializing in digital and mobile financial services, transactional and payment infrastructure.
