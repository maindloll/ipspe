W

hereas standard payment schemes (Visa, MasterCard, etc) are widely
demanded and accepted, there are also new ways that customers use to pay the merchant. Sometimes these are payment proxies that simply mask standard payment scheme by convenient authorisation mechanism, sometimes these are technologically advanced, alternative payment methods that were not viable before “digital revolution”.

Alternative payment methods are especially effective in a niche situation, and as a custom, specific solution for various merchants, such as DCB is a payment method of choice of various parking operators, whereas various wallet-based methods are ideal for proprietary payment systems.

IPSP.de supports a number of wallets, e-currencies and payment methods. All of these methods, unless not noted differently, are provided both online, and at POS.

M

ore than 1 billion people use WeChat and AliPay each month. Both payment methods are based on mobile-based application either providing customer wallet ID to merchant in the form of QR, or scanning merchant QR ID. Transactions are performed on-line, as fast as any classic payment scheme.

Payment scenario

Customer scans a dynamic QR online or at POS with their WeChat or AliPay mobile app and confirms payment with a PIN or biometric authorization.

What IPSP offers

  • • AliPay and WeChat support on IPSP POS terminals
  • • Online payment gateway

B

rick-and-mortar retailers are much closer to the final consumer than any bank will ever be. Banks keep closing down branches and shutting down ATMs. In some places, on the contrary, banks cannot yet arrive at all the places they are needed.

This is an opportunity for retailers to become providers of financial services. Simply speaking, IPSP Digital Financial Service technology helps a corner-shop or a gas station to offer the most common services of a bank branch, or a money remittance point.

This means more visitors and more revenue.

Licences and consulting

In many cases, these services require a government license. Wherever possible, we'll arrange a possibility to work under ours or our partner's licence (so you can operate as before). Where a separate license is needed, our experts will guide you through every step of the process.

Requirements:

  • • IPSP POS-terminal
  • • IPSP must be your acquiring partner

Deployment checklist

  • 1. Service contract signed.
  • 2. Incentive mechanics approved.
  • 3. Approve plastic card design.
  • 4. Loyalty functionality enabled on iPSP POS-terminals.
  • 5. Plastic cards and POS materials arrive by post to each store.
  • 6. 30 minutes web-based training for the front-line staff.

Requirements:

Be a licenced mobile operator or an MVNO.
